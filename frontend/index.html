User Guide

Which menu should I use?

  • Reset Password: Use when you forget your current password and cannot log in.
  • Unlock Account: Use when your account is locked after too many incorrect password attempts.
  • Enrollment: Use when you know your password and want to view your details or update your personal recovery email.

How to Reset Your Password

  1. Click on the Reset Password menu.
  2. Enter your Username and click "Request OTP".
  3. Check your personal/recovery email for a 6-digit OTP.
  4. Enter the OTP and your desired new password on the next screen.
  5. Click confirm, and your password will be changed instantly.
